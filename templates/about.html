About AgriConnect

AgriConnect is a community-driven web platform that bridges the gap between local farmers and customers. We promote sustainable agriculture, support rural livelihoods, and provide customers access to farm-fresh produce - directly, quickly, and affordably.

🌱 Our Mission

Empower local farmers by giving them a digital marketplace to showcase and sell their products - without the dependency on middlemen or commission-based platforms.

🌍 Our Vision

Build a future where food is fresh, local, and transparent - a future where technology uplifts small-scale farmers, and customers know exactly where their food comes from.

✨ What Makes AgriConnect Special?

📦 Direct Marketplace

Farmers list and manage their own products. No middlemen, no cuts - just fair trade.

👨‍🌾 Role-Based Dashboards

Different dashboards for Farmers, Customers, and Admins ensure simplicity and relevance.

⚙️ Clean Tech Stack

Powered by Flask (Python), MySQL, Bootstrap - clean code, session handling, and fully deployable.

💻 Built with Passion

AgriConnect is built with care for both logic and user experience. It’s responsive, scalable, and made for real-world use, combining solid backend architecture with an intuitive frontend.
